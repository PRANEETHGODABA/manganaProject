About Us

Pioneers in the Pyrolysis Industry, leading the way in sustainable recycling.

Who We Are

Manojana Pyrogold India Private Limited is the pioneer of Pyrolysis Industry in Vishakhapatnam, Andra Pradesh, with Government approval for recycling rubber and plastic scrap. Established in September 2021, we have grown from one unit to eight units of pyrolysis plants within a few years, becoming one of the leading manufacturers in the industry.
