Benefits of Tyre Pyrolysis

Our recycling system offers significant environmental and economic advantages.

  • 100% recycling of tyre wastage with no rubber left after the process.
  • Environmentally friendly process with no chemical ingredients used.
  • No soil, water, or air pollution during or after the process.
  • Derives valuable energy resources in the form of oil & char.
  • Each recycled ton preserves 10 tons of CO and reduces pollution.
  • Process can be applied to all rubber-based materials.

Pyrolysis Oil (Tyre Oil)

Pyrolysis oil is a high-quality synthetic fuel derived from waste plastics and tyres. It serves as a cost-effective and more environmentally friendly alternative to industrial fuels like furnace oil and LDO.

  • High Calorific Value: Comparable to diesel, providing excellent energy output.
  • Low Sulphur Content: Results in fewer emissions and is better for the environment.
  • Versatile Applications: Used in boilers, furnaces, hot water generators, and power generation.

Black Carbon Powder

A valuable co-product of the pyrolysis process, our Black Carbon Powder is a high-grade material with numerous industrial applications. It is an excellent substitute for traditional carbon black.

  • Reinforcing Agent: Enhances the strength and durability of rubber products, including new tyres, conveyor belts, and hoses.
  • Pigment: Used as a black pigment in plastics, paints, and inks.
  • Conductive Filler: Can be used in the production of conductive plastics and other materials.

Burned Tyre Wires (Steel Scrap)

The steel recovered from tyres during pyrolysis is a high-tensile, high-purity scrap metal. It provides significant economic benefits for the steel manufacturing industry.

  • Cost-Effective: Acts as a direct substitute for expensive pig iron in steel melting units.
  • Energy Saving: Its properties can help reduce the energy required in the melting process.
  • High Quality: Free from impurities often found in other scrap metals, leading to better quality steel.
